## About Alright

Alright is a mental health tracking app that sends gentle check-ins throughout your day. Just tap an emoji to log your mood, energy, and anxiety — it takes 3 seconds.

- **Privacy-first**: Your data stays on your device and your iCloud
- **Effortless**: Check-ins come to you via notifications
- **AI insights**: Weekly summaries highlight patterns you might miss
- **Therapy-ready**: Generate PDF summaries for your therapist
